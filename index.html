7-Day Workout Plan

Day 1

  • 30 minutes of jogging
  • 3 sets of 15 squats
  • 3 sets of 20 crunches
  • 3 sets of 10 push-ups
  • 1-minute plank

Day 2

  • 30 minutes of cycling
  • 3 sets of 15 lunges (each leg)
  • 3 sets of 20 Russian twists
  • 3 sets of 15 tricep dips

Day 3

  • 20 minutes HIIT workout (alternating 1 minute of sprinting with 1 minute of walking)
  • 3 sets of 15 burpees
  • 3 sets of 20 leg raises
  • 1-minute plank

Day 4

  • 30 minutes brisk walking
  • 3 sets of 15 push-ups
  • 3 sets of 15 side planks (each side)
  • 3 sets of 20 mountain climbers

Day 5

  • 30 minutes swimming
  • 3 sets of 15 squats
  • 3 sets of 20 bicycle crunches
  • 1-minute plank

Day 6

  • 20 minutes HIIT workout
  • 3 sets of 15 lunges
  • 3 sets of 20 leg raises
  • 3 sets of 15 push-ups

Day 7

  • 30 minutes of jogging or brisk walking
  • 3 sets of 15 burpees
  • 3 sets of 20 Russian twists
  • 1-minute plank
